My dear daughter

You have been away from me long enough, and you still choose distance as if it were easier than closeness. This is how you thank your father.... All I have to say is that if the world does not contain you as much as they are, then my heart welcomes you and I will continue to wait for you. Do not procrastinate in being far from your father, for he is dying of longing for you, little one.

Your dear , father
